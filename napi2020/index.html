What is NAPI?

The NAIST Project for Interns (NAPI) offers yearly internship opportunities for students coming from the Ateneo de Manila University's School of Science and Engineering (AdMU-SOSE) in the Philippines.

What is NAIST?

The Nara Institute of Science and Technology (奈良先端科学技術大学院大学 | NAIST) is a school for graduate studies situated in the advanced technology research area of Osaka-Nara-Kyoto.
