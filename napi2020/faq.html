FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Who can apply?
Bona fide students from the Ateneo de Manila University's School of Science and Engineering (SOSE) may apply. They must be:
(1) currently enrolled as an undergraduate student in his/her final year (4th or 5th year);
(2) currently enrolled as a master's degree student; or
(3) B.S. and M.S. graduates who are interested to get a graduate degree in Japan (especially in NAIST).
How to apply for the internship?
See the instructions here . There will also be a briefing about the internship on August 30, 2019 at Ateneo de Manila University.
When is the internship?
The internship will be from 6 - 17 January 2019.
When is the deadline?
The deadline is on 21 September 2018, 11:59PM (Philippine Standard Time).
When will the results be released?
Results will be released mid-October 2018.
How many interns will be accepted?
A total of 5 interns will be accepted.
What if many applicants are interested in one specific lab?
Successful applicants will be assigned to one of their chosen laboratories. However, if for some reason the laboratory you selected is not able to accommodate you, you will be assigned to the one nearest to your field of interest. We recommend that you choose laboratories that match your current research projects/ interests, or can provide technical/research skills that you want to acquire
The lab I am interested in does not provide a theme. Can I still apply?
You may apply for any of the labs in the list of participating labs. You may want to check their website for more details.
What costs are covered by the internship?
NAPI 2019 covers round trip plane fare (Manila - Osaka - Manila), transportation to and from the airport, and accommodations during the internship period.
I have been accepted, what should I do?
You may send an email to your host professor to introduce yourself, and discuss potential topics you will be working on during your internship.
